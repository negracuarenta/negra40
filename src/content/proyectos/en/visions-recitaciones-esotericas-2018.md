**Visions**
esoteric recitations

**Música Naranja**
Pamela Guruciaga and Martín Virgili

*a sound can change our life*

Saturday, July 7, 2018 // 9:00 pm
Estación Araucanía
Av. Bustillo 11.500 – Bariloche

Música Naranja is a duo formed by Martín Virgili and Pamela Guruciaga, primarily interested in exploring new forms of artistic creation from the interaction of body, sound, space and stage. Their repertoire spans vocal works of sound and experimental poetry in dialogue with avant-garde music, performance and theater.

Formed in 2012, they have performed at @ptt (Geneva); Antic Forn de Vallcarca (Barcelona); Rhizome (Washington); Las Fajitas (Nashville); Rutgers University (New Jersey); Casa del Popolo (Montreal); Museo de Arte Contemporáneo de Mar del Plata; Centro Cultural Victoria Ocampo; Club del Teatro (Mar del Plata); Salón de los Representantes; Sala Negra40 and Espacio 6 piso (Buenos Aires, Argentina).

For more information: www.martinvirgili.com.ar
