## Background

Resident since 2013 at the Centro de Investigaciones Artísticas (Buenos Aires), the ensemble has given public performances at the Conservatorio Cristobal Moales in Seville, Spain — alongside Taller Sonoro — at the Conservatoire National Supérieur de Musique et de Danse de Paris, IRCAM (Centre Pompidou), the Museo MUMU (Córdoba, Argentina), the Centro de Experimentación of the Teatro Colón, the Centro de Investigaciones Artísticas, the Centro Cultural Matienzo, the Conservatorio Superior de Música of the City of Buenos Aires, the El Aleph auditorium, and the Sala Villa Villa at the Centro Cultural Recoleta, among other venues in the city of Buenos Aires.

## Ensamble Wonderland!

Its work is centered in the middle ground between musical composition and performance, conceiving of musicians not only as performers but as stage performers with specific musical knowledge, exploring different means of communication between composer and musicians.
