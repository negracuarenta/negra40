## Daniel Forero

Daniel Forero holds a Master of Music / Post-Graduate Diploma in Classical Guitar from the Manhattan School of Music in New York, USA, studying under Maestro Mark Delpriora.

He earned his degree as Master in Music (2005) from the Academia Superior de Artes in Bogotá (ASAB), now the ASAB Faculty of Arts at the Universidad Distrital Francisco José de Caldas, where he studied musical arts with an emphasis in acoustic guitar under Maestra Irene Gómez. He currently teaches full-time at UPTC in Tunja and teaches guitar at the ASAB Faculty of Arts, preparatory level.

To complement his training as a performer and musician, he has taken part in masterclasses and workshops, as well as private lessons, with prominent teachers such as Oscar Ghiglia (Italy), Hopkinson Smith (USA), Carlos Barbosa-Lima (Brazil), Timo Korhonen (Finland), David Leisner (USA), Cristiano Porqueddu (Italy), Denis Azabagic (Bosnia and Herzegovina), Celín and Celino Romero (Spain), Pedro Ángel (Venezuela), Guillermo Bocanegra (Colombia), Guillermo Rendón (Colombia), and violinist Dale Stuckenbruck (USA).
