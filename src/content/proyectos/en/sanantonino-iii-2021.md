**BIENALSUR 2021**

**San Antonino**
Music for orchestras, whistles, cars and yellow boats, for the port of Mar del Plata

Martín Virgili

December 5, 2021 – 11 am — Port of Mar del Plata

Special collaboration in developing the project: Mariano Losi

## Summary

San Antonino is a piece of music composed for the port of Mar del Plata, making a 640,000-square-meter space audible by working the seascape with a variety of sound sources. The project consists of two ensembles of horns and whistles (south and north breakwaters), a car with sirens, four boats fitted with sirens, horns and musicians, and an airplane. A long-range sound system positioned at "el Cristo" on the south breakwater plays back field recordings of the nautical pilgrimage and the port.

San Antonino traces back to the causes behind the construction of a local identity — "the nautical pilgrimage," "the fishermen's feast," "the blessing of the waters" — with a core interest in designing a contemporary popular festival: an art of agreements among peers, an art of peace.

## Motivation

San Antonino is a musical project with a strong heritage imprint, driven by the deteriorating state of Mar del Plata's yellow boats and the port's other heritage assets.

## Movements

*Andante de sirenas con marcato de trompas y silbatos. Vocazioni animate de las escolleras. Proclama emotivo al Puerto de Mar del Plata. Entrata trionfale de los barcos. Passerella a filo de banderas y pañuelos. Danza delle barche con poema a las familias fundadoras. Grande cadenza. Canción del pescador: Madonnina del mare. Homenaje. Omaggio solenne. Amado Mar del Sur, ti ricordiamo con i fiori. Grande finale, maestoso ed esplosivo, con bombas, gritos, coros e luci.*
