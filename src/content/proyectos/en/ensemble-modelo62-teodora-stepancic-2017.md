## Ensemble Modelo62 – Teodora Stepančić

**Concert 1 — Ensemble Modelo62, 2017 Tour**
Wednesday, March 29, 9:00 pm
Teatro Auditorium, Sala Piazzolla
Boulevard Marítimo 2280, Mar del Plata
Free admission

**Concert 2 — Teodora Stepančić: Contemporary Piano**
Thursday, March 30, 8:30 pm
Centro Cultural Victoria Ocampo
Matheu 1851, Mar del Plata
Admission: $40

To mark the opening of negra40's 2017 season, the Netherlands' Ensemble Modelo62 and Serbian pianist Teodora Stepančić will perform in Mar del Plata for the first time.

Ensemble Modelo62, directed by Argentine conductor Ezequiel Menalled, will give a concert of contemporary and experimental music on the stage of the Sala Piazzolla at Teatro Auditorium.

The program will feature music by Colombian composer Rodolfo Acosta; Argentines Mariano Losi, Santiago Santero, and Martín Virgili; Canadian Justin Christensen; and American composer Alvin Lucier.

The concert will also feature the LASO duo — Yamandú Rodríguez and Adriana Sasali — Leopoldo Juanes, and other musicians from Mar del Plata.

Teodora Stepančić, for her part, will perform at the Microcine of Centro Cultural Victoria Ocampo, with a program including works by Mark Taylor, Christian Wolff, Jürg Frey, and Morton Feldman.

## About Ensemble Modelo62

Ensemble Modelo62 is a chamber group devoted to promoting, creating, and performing experimental contemporary music. Offering audiences concerts full of intensity and energy, Ensemble Modelo62 combines commissions from young composers as well as more established names in the genre. The ensemble maintains an open-minded approach to a wide range of aesthetic currents, since building its own tailor-made repertoire is one of its central artistic goals. It also regularly organizes seminars, educational projects, and concerts featuring music by composition students from various institutions, to support the development of young composers.

Over the past six years, Ensemble Modelo62 has established itself as one of the leading ensembles for the performance of avant-garde music, giving numerous concerts every year, both within the Netherlands and internationally.

The ensemble has performed at festivals such as Nu Music (Stavanger, Norway), New Works Festival (Southampton, England), Darmstadt Festival für Neue Musik (Darmstadt, Germany), November Music (Den Bosch, Netherlands), and Gaudeamus Muziekweek (Utrecht, Netherlands), where it will be ensemble-in-residence for the 2016 edition. It has also strengthened its international ties through tours in Argentina (2013), Germany (2010), Belgium (2013), England (2010), Mexico (2014), and Norway (2011).

In 2014 the ensemble released the album "Multiple Paths" in collaboration with the Orpheus Institute (Ghent, Belgium), and in 2015 the Dutch label Attacca released its latest album, "Modelo Moves," featuring music by Argentine and Dutch composers.

Its most recent visit to Argentina was in 2015, to perform the chamber opera "El limonero real" — by its director Ezequiel Menalled, based on the novel of the same name by Juan José Saer — at the Centro de Experimentación of the Teatro Colón.

More information about the ensemble: [www.modelo62.com](http://www.modelo62.com)

## About Teodora Stepančić

Born in Belgrade, Serbia, Teodora Stepančić lives in Brooklyn, New York.

She is a pianist, composer, and curator specializing in the performance of new music. In recent years she has been collecting and performing piano works that speak in the voice of the outsider — a quiet voice that does not seek to draw attention to itself, and that emphasizes generosity, attentiveness, intimacy, spirituality, and concentration.

She is a member of Ensemble Modelo62, Studio (RS), and the duo Stepančić-Gidron. Teodora studied piano and composition at the University of Arts in Belgrade and at the Royal Conservatory in The Hague.

More information: [teodora.stepancic.com](http://teodora.stepancic.com)
