We are very happy to welcome Colombian composer Daniel Leguizamón.

We have prepared two concerts so we can keep discovering his projects, and as a good opportunity to get closer to a refined researcher of Latin American experimentalism.

## Concert 1 – *En los tiempos*: talk-concert

Thursday 6/22 – 7:00 pm
Room 27, Conservatorio Manuel de Falla
Gallo 238 – CABA

Featuring students from the Contemporary Music Performance program, from the classes of Malena Levin and Inés Sabatini, and from the Advanced Diploma in Contemporary Music.

## Concert 2 – *En los tiempos*

Friday 6/23, 8:00 pm (sharp)
Estudio Desvío Temporal
Dr. Guillermo Rawson 930, Morón

An experimental concert together with Daniel Leguizamón (Colombia).
World premiere of "Ventana con flores" ("Window with Flowers")
And a set by UDLI (La utilidad de lo inútil / The Usefulness of the Useless).

*In collaboration with Ocho Ojos Producciones

- Maxi Mas
- Marcelo Von Schultz
