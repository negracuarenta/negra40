*Art and territory in Porto, Portugal*

We designed a circuit linking the mouth of the Douro River to the Atlantic Ocean, in the city of Porto, Portugal.

The Douro — the Duero, the river of gold — closes ranks with the Atlantic after crossing the "Douro Valley," a wine-growing region declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
