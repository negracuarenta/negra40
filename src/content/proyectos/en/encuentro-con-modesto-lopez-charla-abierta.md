**Modesto López**
Open talk

Friday, May 16, 2014, 8:30 pm
negra40 MdP (Playa Grande)

On Friday at 8:30 pm, Modesto López, director of the Mexican record label "Ediciones Pentagrama S.A.," gave an open talk. Modesto, with an extensive career as a producer and documentary filmmaker, spoke to us about his life and about the music industry in Latin America.
