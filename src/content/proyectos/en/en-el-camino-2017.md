***On the Road***
Experimental sound design

*"I heard an indescribable seething murmur that was not in my ear but everywhere, and had nothing to do with sound"*

**Mariano Losi, Adriana Sasali, Martín Virgili**
Electronics and sound mechanics

Jack Kerouac's novel *On the Road* (1949-51) is a story of time, or of time as story. It was written in a '48 Buick convertible in which the writer joined the two coasts of the United States, fueled by whiskey and pills.

The novel stages a visual and sonic time: everything in it walks, marches, pushes forward.

The event *On the Road* is inspired by that particular way of driving through that shape of time.

A time heading west, toward the wild.
