> ##### *"Art, instead of being an object made by one person, is a process set in motion by a group of people. Art is socialized."*
> ###### — John Cage

On August 29, 2022, it will be 70 years since the premiere of *4:33*. Drawing on the resonance of that music, we will gather to share stories of silences, and to perform the piece.

### Places and contacts to take part

- **8:00 pm. Dante Platz. Heidelberg** (Germany). Contact: Martín Virgili and Victoria Salas.
- **7:00 pm. Espacio Balboa. Buenos Aires** (Argentina). Contact: Mariano Losi.
- **10:00 am. Conservatorio Municipal de Música Manuel de Falla. Buenos Aires** (Argentina). Contact: Inés Sabatini.
- **8:00 pm. INSTI. Mar del Plata** (Argentina). Contact: Facundo Passeri.
- **7:00 pm. Finca Las Lomitas. La Vega, Cundinamarca** (Colombia). Contact: Guillermo Bocanegra.
- **5:00 pm. EFF. Casilda, Santa Fe** (Argentina). Contact: Gustavo Frittegotto.
- **4:33. Moosdorfstraße. Berlin** (Germany). Contact: Martí Ruíz.

**All times correspond to the local time of each city.**
