Art and territory in Heidelberg

A participatory piece built around the Neckar river, in Heidelberg, Baden-Württemberg, Germany.

We designed a circuit meant to be walked ten times as the sun goes down.

## Movements

- *A la natura*
- *Scala al ponte*
- *Gran giro entrata al Alte Brücke*
- *Subida Imperiale*
- *Salida al sotoportego del caballo de los suspiros*
- *Homenaje a Deleuze: flujo y corte*
- *Caminata a la vera del Castillo*
- *Rampa dei coraggiosi*
- *Gran Canal*
- *Pirouette finale sobre el Neckar*

## July 12, 7 pm

Heidelberg city center

**Duration:** 10 laps (3.5 hours)

**Materials:** whistles (or other loud sound devices), a flashlight, flowers, water, comfortable clothing.
