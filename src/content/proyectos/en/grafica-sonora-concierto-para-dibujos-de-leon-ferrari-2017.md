**Sound Graphics**
A concert for two drawings by León Ferrari.

Western music has long maintained a relationship of technical and aesthetic exchange with drawing that has never been emphatically described — one that the musical experimentalism of the twentieth century was finally able to take up, expanding the graphic possibilities of the score. This expansion also reverberated through the visual arts, which found themselves able to imagine a music from within their own ontological field.

This concert offers a glimpse of the music imagined by artists who, without being musicians, nonetheless know how to draw it.
