**Los últimos / The Last Ones (2023)**
Documentary film

Commissioned by the Centro de Arte y Ciencia (CEAC), UTN Mar del Plata

A 46-minute documentary portraying the life of the last artisanal fishermen of the traditional yellow boats of Mar del Plata, a symbol of the city's identity.
