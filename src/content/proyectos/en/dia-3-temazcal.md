An ancestral ceremony of purification for body, mind, emotions, and spirit through heat, water, and steam. Honoring the moment of conception and the creation of the whole universe, the oldest memory, the first movement where life occurred, and the origin of the first breath. A ritual of heated stones in the womb of our mother earth, originating with the Native Peoples of Mesoamerica. Led by Mariano Losi.
