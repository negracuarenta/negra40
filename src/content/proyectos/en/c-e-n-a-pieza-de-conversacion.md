#### *Conversation piece*

How far can the force produced by an unexpected crossing go? Two voices that never sounded together — or more, three or four? Hearing oneself through what someone else says? Can we intone an idea, sing a thought, a feeling, sheltered by the warmth of a gathering brought together for the occasion? **C.E.N.A** — *Conversation Piece* — sketches out the conditions for a group of people who feel powerful to gather, look at one another, affect one another, and compose, as much as we can, the collective fabric of a discussion about our present, art, and the future.
