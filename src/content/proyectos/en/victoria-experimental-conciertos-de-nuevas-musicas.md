## About the concert

The concert brings together the work of two contemporary music composers and a visual artist, each oriented, in their own way, toward an exploration of sound, time, and musical space. These are experimental musics in the double sense of the word: on the one hand, because they themselves are unaware of the aesthetic possibilities of their own unfolding, and on the other, because they somehow lead the audience to revisit its (mythical and historical) assumptions about the experience of sound.
