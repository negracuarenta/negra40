**negra 40 presents**

**Trio Stéphane Pécas**

**Geneviève Liboiron, violin**
**Émilie Girard-Charest, cello**
**Daniel Áñez, piano**

As part of its tour of the Southern Cone, the Canadian trio will perform in Buenos Aires, at our venue, with a concert program that promises much.

Those interested should write to [email protected]

**Thursday, August 21, 8:30 pm**
**Sala negra40, CABA (Palermo)**

Trio Stéphane Pécas is made up of three of the most active musicians of the new generation of contemporary music in Montreal, Canada. Members of active ensembles such as Paramirabo, Allogène, and La Machine, the members of Stéphane Pécas are once again touring South America, giving concerts in Santiago, Santa Fe, Montevideo, Buenos Aires, and La Paz, and taking part in several festivals and album launches.

## Full tour itinerary

- August 1–8 — International Composers' Meeting, Santiago, Chile
- August 11–15 — Festival Sonido Presente, Santa Fe, Argentina
- August 18 — Núcleo de Música Nueva de Montevideo, Sala Zitarrosa, Montevideo, Uruguay
- August 19 (to be confirmed) — Launch of the Gourmet Musical book on Graciela Paraskevaídis, Buenos Aires, Argentina
- August 21 — Negra40, Buenos Aires, Argentina
- August 25–29 — Meetings with composer Cergio Prudencio, La Paz, Bolivia

The tour repertoire includes works by Argentine composers Gerardo Gandini, Graciela Paraskevaídis, Natalia Solomonoff, Martín Virgili, Mariano Rocca, and Diego Taranto; by Canadian composers Émilie Girard-Charest, Joane Hétu, and Analía Llugdar; and also works by Cergio Prudencio, Beat Furrer, Olga Neuwirth, Nicolaus A. Huber, and Giacinto Scelsi.
